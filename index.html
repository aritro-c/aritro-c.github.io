Current PhD Research

Focus: Coupled Atmosphere/Mantle Evolution with Late Accretion Impact Sequences[cite: 62, 63].

My research utilizes the advanced 3D capabilities of the StagYY code to investigate how late accretion (LA) — the delivery of meteoric material after core formation — influenced the divergent evolution of Earth and Venus[cite: 71, 72].
